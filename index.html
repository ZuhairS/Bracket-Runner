Customizable Bracket Creator

This is an app made for gamers, by gamers. We understand that you want the ability to customize and structure your brackets to your liking. BracketRunner gives you full control of how your tournament goes down.

Notifications

Want to know exactly when and where your favorite pro player or team is competing in a match? BracketRunner gives you the option to follow players & teams, and notifies you when they're in a game. We'll even link you to the match stream if it's available!

Profiles

User profiles provide rich information, including all social media links and a customizable avatar. You can even click through to a player's profile straight from a tournament bracket.
